Quarantining With Bri

Today is the first day of the 3rd week of quarantine. It's been extended through the end of April. Life is weird. There's no one outside. Going to the grocery store means risking illness. There seems to be a crazy sense of togetherness online though and it's been getting me through. I love the random free in home concerts and DNICE DJ sets. It's definitely lonely but it's going to be okay. Ordered Buy 1 Get 1 Free Chipotle burrito bowls. life is okay.

I am sick of being in this apartment. All of my neighbors are a mess. The people upstairs are so loud, The people next door are always playing instruments. The weather is starting to get nice, I just want to go outside. I also want to go grocery shopping and not feel like im going to die. Some positives: Im healthy, I bought starburst jelly beans, I am going to dye eggs and make a nice Easter dinner, I finally have time to relax a bit.

I'm actually terrible at keeping up with this.
It's been exactly a month and not too much has changed in the world, everything seems kind of normal. It seems like the death rate from COVID is steadily decreasing & positive cases don't seem to be exponentially increasing anymore.
